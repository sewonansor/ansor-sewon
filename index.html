About Ansor Organisation

Founded in 2010, Ansor Organisation has been at the forefront of community development and environmental conservation.

Our mission is to empower communities, protect natural resources, and foster sustainable development through collaborative efforts and innovative programs.

With over 100 active projects across 15 countries, we've impacted the lives of more than 50,000 people through our various initiatives.
